My name is Trevor. I'm passionate about web development and all the technologies that make it possible. My favorite hobby is also what I do for a living, which is great. I'm the head web developer at [Jolt](https://joltup.com). I enjoy learning from all the great developers around me and hope to offer what knowledge I have in turn.

## Why another blog?
Web development is a super exciting field, full of smart people making incredible things. Web technologies are evolving so quickly, it's hard to stay current with the latest and greatest. What's even harder is for us mere mortals to understand what the super geniuses behind these technologies have created.

My goal is to explore and explain these technologies in as simple terms as I can. There are many other good sources out there, and I hope to offer a useful perspective. In the worst case scenario, this blog will be a place for me to store notes. In the best case scenario, I hope it will help someone else out.
