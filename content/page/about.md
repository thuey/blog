My name is Trevor. I'm passionate about web development and all the technologies that make it possible. My favorite hobby is also what I do for a living, which is great. I'm the head web developer at [Jolt](https://joltup.com). I enjoy learning from all the great developers around me, and hope to share what I've learned in turn.

Few things can beat the feeling of understanding something so well that you can predict its behavior and very little surprises you. When you start learning a new technology, it's typical for it to seem like magic. Trying to debug an issue is just throwing things at the wall to see what sticks. With time, though, you come to understand the new technology better and the surprises become less and less frequent. That's my end goal with each technology I work in, and I think it's hard to be really effective until you reach that point: no surprises.
